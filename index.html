About Me

Hey there! My name is Max and I am a fresh face on the development scene. I started self-teaching a few months into 2019 with technologies like HTML CSS and Javascript before enrolling in a bootcamp through the Trilogy extension at UCSD. Learning under Travis, John, and Clark, my cohort and I are rapidly expanding our familiarity with web development and I am eager to see what is still yet to come!

I was born and raised in the Boston area of Massachusettes but decided to move out to San Diego when I was just around 20 years old with a good friend of mine from high school named Zak. We rented a Penske truck, threw our stuff in the back, and drove across the country to find our way to the sunny beaches of California. I have been enjoying the delightfully mild weather here for over 12 years now and thoughts of regret for making that move have never entered my mind.

My interests are varied, ranging from sports like soccer, wrestling or even just hiking to more indoor activities like video games, watching a good show or movie, and occasionally cooking with my wife Lydia. I have been married for several years now and we are happily expecting our first born by the end of 2019!

Thanks for checking out my site and I hope you enjoy your time here. Feel free to hit me up with a message via the contact page or head over to my portfolio to see what I have worked on so far along my travels towards becoming a competant coder/developer.
